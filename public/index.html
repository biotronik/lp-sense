True Atrial Signal, No Atrial Lead

BIOTRONIK’s DX system offers complete atrial diagnostics in a single lead ICD, while reducing lead complications often associated with dual-chamber ICDs2. As demonstrated in the SENSE Trial1, the DX system provides electrophysiologists with the technology to enable better management of atrial arrhythmias in their patients.
